<The remainder>
Your net worth to the world is usually determined by what remains after your bad habits are divided by your good ones.
BENJAMIN FRANKLIN, circa 1760

There's another arithmetic operator that may be less familiar to you: the remainder, represented by the % symbol. It just gives you the remainder of a division between two numbers. 

38 % 10; // returns 8
21 % 3; // returns 0

  

 

 
There are a lot of applications for this operator besides simply finding the remainder of a division. try thinking about some of them before completing this exercise.
